### 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a **Full Stack Developer** from India who enjoys building across the entire stack — from crafting polished frontends to writing authentication engines and even diving into systems programming with Rust. My projects range from e-commerce platforms and AI assistants to card games and brain tumor detection models.

- 🔭 Currently building **invent** & **invent-monorepo** (TypeScript + Rust)
- 🧠 Working on **auth-engine** — a custom authentication system
- 🤖 Contributing to **openclaw** — an open-source AI assistant
- ⚡ I solve problems on **LeetCode** & **GeeksForGeeks** in my free time
